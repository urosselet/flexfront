About Us

Flexcrowd helps people find a crowdsourcing platform which best meets their needs and make key decisions in the process. It is based on existing research and data about dozens of crowdsourcing websites. Choosing cards corresponding to key decisions of the crowdsourcing process allows for preparation of the operation and filtering of the list of websites.

The Flexcrowd research project (coordinated by the Management Department of the University of Applied Sciences and Arts of Western Switzerland Arc - HEG-Arc) has been possible thanks to the support of the RCSO ISNET.
